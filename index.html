01 · About

A bit about me

I'm a Senior Product Designer with 22 years of experience — started in 2004 with a non-stop journey across Press, Editorial, Web, Gaming, and Mobile. I've worked on-site for major companies and remotely with startups, as well as a freelancer across LATAM, USA, and Israel.

In recent years I've focused on UX/UI for Mobile, Product, and Gaming, and expanded into Web3 — most recently at Big Time Studios / OpenLoot, bridging blockchain infrastructure and intuitive marketplace UX. As a former Creative Leader, I've managed multidisciplinary teams of up to 12, optimizing workflows that directly drive business growth.

Based in Buenos Aires, Argentina · UBA graduate · Available for remote freelance and full-time opportunities worldwide.
